Who We Are

At Wood Fired Designs, we help ambitious founders bring bold ideas to life through strategic branding and powerful visual storytelling. We’re not just designers; we’re partners in your business growth. With deep roots in identity design, merchandise, and packaging, we craft brands that don’t just look good, they mean something. Whether you’re launching a startup or leveling up your legacy, we blend strategy and creativity to make sure your brand stands out, connects deeply, and sells confidently. No fluff. No filler. Just purpose-built design that lights a fire under your vision.

Behind the Brand

Wood Fired Designs was built from the belief that great design should do more than just look good, it should work hard, tell the truth, and spark real connection. Founded by Michael Deschenes, a strategist and designer with a love for meaningful brands and no patience for generic templates, this studio exists to help entrepreneurs build something they’re proud of. Every project is personal. Every brand has a backbone. We bring clarity, edge, and intention to the table, so your brand isn’t just another log on the fire. It’s the one that starts it.
